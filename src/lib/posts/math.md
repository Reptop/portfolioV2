### My History With Math 
As a kid, I hated math because I was terrible at it and didn't think I was smart enough to understand it. My parents decided to put me in those tutoring centers every Asian kid you know goes to. I hated it at first, but having a teacher not giving up on me and explaining concepts clearly enough for me to understand made me rethink my relationship with math.

### Why I Love Math + CS
I think CS + Math is a perfect combination especially right now. With the word "AI" being said every 1 second at tech companies, conferences, and news media outlets, I think it's important to have a strong foundation in math to understand how these models work and how to use them effectively. I also think that having a strong foundation in math can help you become a better problem solver and critical thinker. Math is also super fun sometimes, like in linear algebra when you visualize linear transformations. Maybe I'm weird, who knows?

### Conclusion
I think anyone can learn math, even if they think they are bad at it. And if you don't really care, then that's cool too! Let's keep pressing buttons on keyboard and make cool things :)
